About

The series "Persistenze" stems from a reflection on the early decades of the contemporary era and and on what continues to endure despite time and transformation. The three photographs move across past, present and future as interconnected states: historical memory resurfacing ("Il peso della memoria"), the attempt to release the weight of inherited stories ("Spogliarsi delle storie"), and a final horizon marked by silent human traces ("Miraggi").

The work observes how individuals, spaces, and structures coexist with what does not disappear—collective traumas, urban messages, technological residues. Photography becomes a means to question the relationship between presence and absence, between what we move through and what moves through us.
