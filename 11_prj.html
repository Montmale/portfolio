About

"El hombre liberado del orden del tiempo" is a short film I directed during my Erasmus in Barcelona. The film explores the theme of time, flowing silently through the neighborhood during the hot midday hours.

The camera remains still, while the narrative rhythm is driven by the spontaneous movement of subjects entering and leaving the frame; it is filmed in reverse, suggesting the idea of temporal distortion. The soundtrack, "Tristan und Isolde" by Wagner, amplifies the sense of unease and introspection with its suspended harmonies and emotional tension.

The title, drawn from Marcel Proust's "In Search of Lost Time", embodies the struggle to break free from the inescapable flow of time.
