About

The short film was created using Blender, a 3D modeling and animation software. The narrative style is inspired by Studio Ghibli.

Totoro, in his pirate guise, is forced to face harsh weather conditions during one of his adventures: rain, rough seas, and strong winds. After overcoming the storm, he reaches his destination, a small, peaceful island, where he finds his friends, Chu and Chibi.

The characters, objects, and environments were modeled in a stylized yet realistic and evocative manner. We carefully crafted the materials, color palette, and sound design, as well as the animation of natural phenomena.
