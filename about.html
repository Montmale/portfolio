I’m Alessandra,
an Italian visual designer studying Communication Design at the Polytechnic University of Milan. For me, design is a means of self-expression and a way to connect with the world. The great thing is that it’s everywhere — you just have to open your eyes, listen, and get to work.
Strong emotions, especially negative ones, are often said to be the source of art. But design isn't art. Here, care and passion are the keys. As Albert Einstein once said, “Creativity is intelligence having fun.”
