About

"La Porta | Seaside Welcome Center" is a brand project created for the Portal de la Pau building at the Port of Barcelona, transformed into a welcoming space for those arriving by sea.

La Porta symbolizes the act of opening — your world, your senses, and your connection to Barcelona’s true culture. As the first step into the city, La Porta guides visitors through authentic experiences, uncovering local traditions and hidden gems beyond typical tourist spots.

Developed during my Erasmus exchange, the project includes the creation of a complete visual identity: from brand positioning to logo design, motion design, typography, digital applications, and wayfinding.
