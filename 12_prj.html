About

I am passionate about photography, a world I have explored especially in recent years. Since I was a child, I have felt the need to freeze time to admire what surrounds me, from vast landscapes to small details — first through drawing, now through my shots.

I love immersing myself in new and stimulating environments. It is during my travels that I often find inspiration for my photographs, letting myself be guided by the emotions that arise from encounters with others.

My favorite subjects are natural landscapes and the human body. When I capture them, I try to convey the sensations I feel — the ones that the earth, the sea, the sky, the skin, and a person's gaze communicate to me.
