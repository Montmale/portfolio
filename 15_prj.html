Rebranding

2025

→ rebranding of a luxury perfume brand based in Monaco, developed with CONO Studio

About

Luxury branding is often built on codes of perfection, heritage and exclusivity, where visual identity becomes a tool to reinforce desirability and status. This rebranding project explores how a contemporary visual language can reinterpret luxury without relying on excess or nostalgia.

The work investigates the balance between tradition and modernity, translating the identity of a place into a refined yet distinctive system. Through typography, color and material sensibility, the project aims to create a visual narrative that feels timeless, gender-neutral and recognisable.

The goal is to design an identity that doesn't simply decorate the product, but strengthens its meaning, coherence and presence across physical and digital touchpoints.
